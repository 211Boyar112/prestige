4. GAMSTOP – Multi-Operator Self-Exclusion

GAMSTOP is a free UK-wide self-exclusion scheme that allows you to exclude from all online gambling companies licensed in Great Britain.

4.1 How GAMSTOP Works

  • Register once at www.gamstop.co.uk
  • Choose your exclusion period (6 months, 1 year, or 5 years)
  • Your exclusion will apply to all UK licensed operators
  • Registration is free and takes about 24 hours to activate

4.2 What GAMSTOP Covers

Once registered with GAMSTOP, you will be prevented from:

  • Accessing gambling websites and apps of UK licensed operators
  • Opening new accounts with these operators
  • Receiving promotional materials from these operators

Note: GAMSTOP only covers online gambling with UK licensed operators. It does not cover non-UK operators, betting shops, or casinos.

5. Signs of Problem Gambling

Gambling becomes a problem when it affects your life negatively. Here are some warning signs to watch for:

Personal Warning Signs

  • Spending more time or money on gambling than you can afford
  • Chasing losses (trying to win back money you've lost)
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to gamble
  • Lying to family or friends about gambling activities
  • Neglecting work, studies, or family responsibilities
  • Feeling restless or irritable when not gambling
  • Gambling to escape problems or negative emotions
  • Failed attempts to cut down or stop gambling

5.1 Financial Warning Signs

  • Difficulty paying bills or meeting financial obligations
  • Using credit cards or loans for gambling
  • Overdrafts or unpaid bills due to gambling
  • Borrowing from friends or family for gambling

5.2 Behavioral Warning Signs

  • Spending increasing amounts of time gambling
  • Gambling instead of attending to responsibilities
  • Concealing gambling activities from others
  • Gambling when feeling depressed, anxious, or stressed
